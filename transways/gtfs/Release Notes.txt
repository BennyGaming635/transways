Notice for Developers:

We are planning on updating our GTFS feed on 2 June 2026.

We will be adding a new booking_rules.txt file. The file will include the following fields. 

booking_rule_id,booking_type,prior_notice_last_day,prior_notice_last_time,message

Our stop_times.txt file will also be updated with the additional fields:

pickup_booking_rule_id,drop_off_booking_rule_id

This follows official GTFS specification. Please ensure your imports will be able to handle the new data.
